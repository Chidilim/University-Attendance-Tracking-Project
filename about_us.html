Meet The Trackmates

Created by Maahum, the Trackmates are a group of friendly robots that love candy, slip 'n' slides, and students that attend their classes. Get to know each of their personalities, and feed them a candy each time you attend a class!

Bluey

Bluey is the lead trackmate. His favorite activity is helping students log in, and helping his Trackmate friends. He also loves apples and sunny days.

Fluffle

Fluffle was the first ever trackmate Maahum created. He loves marshmallows, cotton candy, and watching the clouds float by. He is a shy trackmate.

Bubblegum

Bubblegum is a cuddly trackmate. She loves giving her Trackmate friends hugs, soaking in the sunlight, and making new friends. Her favorite day is Friday.

Rocket

Rocket is a very fiery trackmate. He loves rockets, adventures, and Guardians Of The Galaxy. He only eats sour gummy worms and protein shakes, and steals Fluffle's marshmallows.

Skip

Skip is very confused but he's happy to be here. He's not sure what's going on but he loves helping out his friends! In Skip's free time, he blows bubbles and goes for a swim.

Frodo

Frodo is a curious trackmate. He likes to learn, give things a try, and hear stories. He is very thoughtful and a little bit introverted. He loves sandwiches and grass.
